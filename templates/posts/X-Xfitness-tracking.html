Tracking Fitness Progress With Open Source Apps

While there are a lot of open source fitness and diet tracking apps, not all of them may be suitable for ones needs. Here I will document my experiences with each app I test off of F-Droid.

For context: this whole "review" will be done in my perspective but I will try to state the objective truth about these apps before giving my own experience review. My workout currently consists only of calisthenics and I will be focusing on that aspect for the most part, however I will probably talk about viability for weightlifting too.

List of contents:

  • Massive
  • wger
  • Fast N Fitness
  • Splitfit
  • Lift
  • WorkoutLog
  • Conclusion

I will use each app for 10 sessions in total. This testing may not show how the app feels in the long term, however I simply can not sit on my phone for 30 minutes inputting my workout details into 6 apps after every session, so it is what it is.
